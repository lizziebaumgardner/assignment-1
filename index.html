As a native to Northeast Queens, Ben Chou is running for city council to represent District 19. This includes Bayside, Auburndale, College Point, Whitestone, Bay Terrace, Beechhurst, parts of Flushing, Little Neck and Douglaston. With his deep connection to the area, Chou is running on the primary tenets of

community, affordability and safety.
